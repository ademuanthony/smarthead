About us

Remoteschool.com.ng is a cloud-based learning platform that offers safe and affordable interactive online and home (private tutoring). Our platform is designed to help teachers deliver content to learners, by taking advantage of the power of technology and making learning worthwhile, fun and more engaging. It takes the best of what technology has to offer and deliver effective results for both teachers and students alike.

This platform was founded by a team of ICT and education experts who have the 21st century skills needed to deliver content and pedagogy. It is a cutting edge technology that assists your child learn with 99% guaranteed satisfaction.

We bring this technology to your doorstep. Your child does not need the presence of a home teacher to learn. With the aid of an internet connection and a laptop or a mobile phone, your child would be paired to a tutor who will have live sessions with your child within an agreed time. That simple? Yes!!! Our team of teachers are experts with more than five(5) years of teaching experience and well disciplined in their area of specialization.
