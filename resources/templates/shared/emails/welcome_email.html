How to join the class

You can join the class using a computer, tablet or mobile phone. See instruction below

MOBILE PHONES/TABLETS:
      Download Microsoft Teams from Playstore  and install on your phone
      Login to https://www.smarthead.com.ng using the details provided above.
      Click Join Classroom on the lesson you want to join. Note: the button will only appear during the time of the class
      Enter your Name and click Join

You will see a message that says “someone in  the meeting will let you in soon”. At this point wait patiently.

LAPTOP OR DESKTOP COMPUTER:
      Download Google Chrome browser and install (if not already installed) or installed Microsoft Teams  on your computer(from https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/microsoft-teams/download-app
      Login to https://www.smarthead.com.ng using the details provided above.
      Click Join Classroom on the lesson you want to join. Note: the button will only appear during the time of the class
      Click on  use web app instead
      Enter your name and click join

You will see a message that says “someone in the meeting will let you in soon”. At this point wait patiently.
